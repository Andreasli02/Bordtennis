Testing Document

We decided to use live testing of our website to get insight of how users would interact with the site. We did not create any a google form to send out as we decided live testing was a better way of gaining insight. The testers use their own devices for testing such that we would get a varied number of testing platforms. We used a couple general questions for our users while doing live testing if they did not know what to look for. These questions are listed below:

  1. How is the usability of the website?
  2. Is it easy to find and acess contact information?
  3. Are there any functions you miss?
  4. How does teh excisting functions work?
  5. How would you rate the professionalism of the site
  6. Is it a clear cut usage to the page?
  7. How is the structure and design of the webiste?
  8. What catches your eyes first?
  9. Do the color palates fit the page?
  10. Is there a clear call to action on every page?
