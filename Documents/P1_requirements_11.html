P1: Requirements for the website

Authors: Andreas, Ole, Sander, Erlend, and Magnus

Date: 15.09.2021

Client (imaginary): NTNUI Bordtennis

Purpose and goals

The purpose and goals of this website is to be both a connection for table tennis interested students, and the organization, as well as to be an information hub for the organization

Audience

The audience will be students, table tennis enthusiasts, and generally other members of NTNUI

The content of the site and how it is organized

  • Index
    • Newest events (matches, social gatherings)
    • Member scoreboard
    • Become a new member form
  • Intro to table tennis
    • Ruleset
    • Equipment
    • Beginner tips
    • How to become a new member
  • Regular training
    • Time, location and all that
    • Calendar view for this month
  • Social gatherings
    • Pictures

Functional requirements:

  • Form to contact the organization
  • Information
    • Meetings
    • Matches
  • Table tennis fun facts

Non-functional requirements

  • Be a point of contact for new potential members
  • A central information hub for the members

Final location

The project will probably sit at this GitLab page we created for it, to collaborate easier
