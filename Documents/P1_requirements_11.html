Authors: Andreas, Ole, Sander, Erlend, and Magnus

Date: 15.09.2021


Client (imaginary)

NTNUI Bordtennis


Purpose and goals

The purpose of this website is to be both a connection for ping-pong interested students, and the organization, as well as to be an information hub for the organization. Therefore, our primary goal is to provide users with the opportunity to join open training/tryouts, and join the organization, so that the organization can get new active members. therefore, we will provide contact information, and the training schedule in a neat manner.


Audience

The target audience of this website will be potential members looking for a place to meet others with the same interest in table tennis. Existing members acquiring information about future training, competition events, scoreboards and information given by the coaches. As well as organizational committees getting feedback from members and giving out new information.


The content of the site and how it is organized

Landing page that includes both coming events and become a new member form. Or rulebook for tournaments on the same page as organizational requirements

Content hierarchy wise should be similar to NTNUI's website

  • Index
    • Newest events (matches, social gatherings)
    • Member scoreboard
    • Become a new member form
  • Intro to table tennis
    • Here we will include all the different rules present in the sport of table tennis. The rules written here refelcts the official rules of the IITF (international table tennis foundation) as these are the rules used in competition. We ill also include general fairplay rules in the NTNUI table tennis community.
    • The equimpent used in the NTNUI table tennis club will be stated here. It will include information regarding bringing personal equipment, the equpment used in official matches and the equimpent used by NTNUI members.
    • Beginner tips are important for new players wanting to learn and for experienced members going back to the basics. Here users can find a bunch of differnet tips about playing style, grip, equipment, positioning, servs and different strokes. There will also be an faq section where you can veiw commonly asked questions.
    • Lastly there will be information and a link on how to become a new member
  • Regular training
    • Time, location and all that
    • Calendar view for this month
  • Social gatherings
    • Pictures

Functional requirements:

  • Form to sign up to/contact the organization
  • Information
    • Meetings
    • Matches
  • Table tennis fun facts

Non-functional requirements

  • Be a point of contact for new potential members
  • A central information hub for the members
  • The user should find information about the next match in few to no clicks
