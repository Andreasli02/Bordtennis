P1: Requirements for the website

Authors: Andreas, Ole, Sander, Erlend, and Magnus

Date: 15.09.2021


Client (imaginary)

NTNUI Bordtennis


Purpose and goals

The purpose of this website is to be both a connection for ping-pong interested students, and the organization, as well as to be an information hub for the organization. Therefore, our primary goal is to provide users with the opportunity to join open training/tryouts, and join the organization, so that the organization can get new active members. therefore, we will provide contact information, and the training schedule in a neat manner.


Audience

The target audience of this website will be potential members looking for a place to meet others with the same interest in table tennis. Existing members acquiring information about future training, competition events, scoreboards and information given by the coaches. As well as organizational committees getting feedback from members and giving out new information.


The content of the site and how it is organized

Landing page that includes both coming events and become a new member form. Or rulebook for tournaments on the same page as organizational requirements

Content hierarchy wise should be similar to NTNUI's website

  • Index
    • CTA to regular training
    • Newest events (matches, social gatherings)
    • Member scoreboard
    • Button to contact page for new members

    • This will be the landing page for those who visit our website, and we want to instantly give them access to different links depending on what information they're looking for. Firstly they will meet a quick link to the regular training site, where they can see news about when practices will be held and additional information.

    Then an arrangement of information such as upcoming events, and the member scoreboard to give an impression of what this website is useful for, and how active the group is.

    Lastly we will include an easy to spot button that takes the user to the contact page, where they can fill out the form to become a member of the group.

  • Intro to table tennis
    • Rulsets
    • Equipment
    • Beginner tips
    • Tryouts
    • How to become a member

    • Here we will write usefull information to players wanting to join NTNUI table tennis and new members. Rulsets include the official rules given by the international table tennis foundation as these are the rules used in competition. Equipment will include information regarding personal equipment, the equpment used in official matches and the equimpent used by NTNUI members. Beginner tips provides users with information regarding differnet tips about playing style, grip, equipment, positioning, servs and different strokes. There will also be an faq section where you can veiw commonly asked questions. The tryouts section describes how to apply for tryouts and links where potential new members can find more information regarding tryouts. Lastly it will be described how peaople can apply and become new members of NTNUI table tennis. This will also incloude links to regristration.

  • Regular training
    • Time, location and all that
    • Calendar view for this month
    • Upcoming events/tournaments
  • Social gatherings
    • Previous events/tournaments
    • Pictures from excursions
  • Contact
    • Contact information
    • Become new member form

    • The "contact us" page will include contact information both for the coach, and the group's team leader. Both their names, e-mail addresses, and phone numbers will be listed here. This will act as a portal for people with other questions that aren't answered on the website. In this section there will also be a link to the group's facebook page, and the sub-page within NTNUI.

    On this page we will also include an easy-to-access form that you can fill out if you're interested in becoming a member. This form will play a central part in getting new members' information, where they will give us both a phone number, a-mail address, name, and a quick comment on how much experience they already have with table tennis.
